Prior to that role, I was a research assistant in [College of Electrical Engineering](http://ee.zju.edu.cn/englishee/main.htm) at [Zhejiang University](https://www.zju.edu.cn/english/), under the supervision of Prof. [Yanxin Li](https://person.zju.edu.cn/en/EElyx#).
